Registration



NENSA Membership

Every skier needs to be registered with NENSA and have a NENSA member number.

To get a NENSA membership number (needed for Great Brook BKL registration):
  1. Log on to NENSA and create a NENSA account

  2. Register each child ($25 for BKL skiers)

  3. Copy down each NENSA Number and enter them into Great Brook BKL registration form


Great Brook BKL Registration and Payment

Please fill out our [PUT LINK TO REGISTRATION FORM] registration form and follow the payment steps in the form. Make sure you noted the NENSA membership number(s) as these are needed in our registration process.
